Who I am

As I said before my name is Alistair. I am currently in the military, However I plan to transition out soon. Because of this, I am self-studying computer science. But why self study instead of college? I actually have started working towards a computer science degree, but being in the military causes my schedule to be unpredictable. I could wait to continue after I transition, but I thought, why wait? I realized that there are paths in life other than college, especially as a programmer. I could build a project from scratch and put that on a resume, or I could study for a certification.


So that’s my plan—learn, create projects, and document my progress along the way. I hope that you find some value out of this.
