Welcome

Hello, my name is Alistair, and this is my website. After spending five years in the military, I decided to leave and study computer science.

This website is a collection of my projects, goals, and ideas. I focus mainly on tech-related topics, like coding, Linux, and web development. However I also talk about hobbies that interest me such as yoyoing or Rubik's cubes.
