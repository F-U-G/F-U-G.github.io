Welcome

Hello, my name is Alistair, and this is my website. I am currently self-studying computer science.


This website focuses on the skills that I have learned through self-study. I focus mainly on tech-related topics, like coding, Linux, and web development. However I also talk about the hobbies that interest me such as yoyoing or Rubik's cubes. In my blog I go over the basics of what I learned and how I apply it to my projects. If the topic interests you, I provide the resources to get started.
